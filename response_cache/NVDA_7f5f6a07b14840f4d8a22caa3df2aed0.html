0. SUMMARY OF THE STOCK MARKET AND INDUSTRY

  • Main Products/Services:
    Nvidia designs and sells GPUs (graphics processing units) for gaming, data centers and AI, automotive, and professional visualization. Its core products: GeForce GPUs (gaming), NVIDIA RTX (graphics/AI), A100/H100 chips (AI/datacenter), automotive systems (Drive platform), and software (CUDA, Omniverse).

  • Market Size & Growth Potential:

  • Global AI hardware/software market >$100 bn and growing >20% CAGR.
  • Datacenter/AI chip market is projected to exceed $200 bn by 2028.
  • Nvidia’s TAM is expanding rapidly with generative AI.

  • Gaming remains robust; automotive segment in early growth phase.

  • Main Competitors:

  • Semiconductors: AMD, Intel, Qualcomm
  • AI/hyperscalers: Google (TPU), Amazon (Inferentia), Apple (custom silicon)

  • Automotive: Mobileye (Intel), Qualcomm, Tesla

  • Main Market Drivers:

  • Surge in AI model training/inference demand (generative AI, LLMs)
  • Cloud/data center investment cycles
  • Gaming console/PC refreshes

  • Edge computing and automotive ADAS/AV adoption

  • Main Market Risks:

  • Valuation bubble and profit-taking
  • Competitive threats (AMD/Google/Apple custom AI chips)
  • Geopolitical tensions (China restrictions)
  • Cyclical slowdowns (datacenter, gaming)
  • Supply chain constraints

La empresa goza de márgenes de utilidad excepcionales, un crecimiento de ingresos y beneficios muy superior al promedio del sector, y mantiene una posición dominante en temas de innovación y cuota de mercado, especialmente dentro del boom de la IA.

El volumen ha disminuido respecto a la media, indicando menor convicción compradora en niveles actuales. Señales técnicas como el “death cross” (cruce bajista de medias) y la sobrecompra estocástica refuerzan la probabilidad de un giro bajista.

Actividad institucional reciente (ej. aumento del stake de CoreWeave) es positiva, pero no compensa riesgos de corrección.
